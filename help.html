1. Move with the arrow keys.
2. If a monster is next to you, move toward it to attack it.
3. If a monster tries to move into you, it will attack you.
4. Hit chests to open them.
5. Click items in your inventory to equip them.
6. Press space to view a map of what you've explored so far.
7. Look out for trapdoors.

Good luck!

P.S. press [s] to toggle smooth lighting
